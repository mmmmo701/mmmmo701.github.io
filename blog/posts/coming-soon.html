Inline Math

Inline formulas stay inside the text flow. For example, the famous energy–mass relation is \(E = mc^2\), which renders inline without breaking the paragraph.

Display Math

The quadratic formula (centered display):

$$ x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} $$

Math is rendered client-side with MathJax. If formulas don't appear, wait a moment for the library to load or ensure JavaScript is enabled.
